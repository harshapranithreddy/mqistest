About MQ Indian Society

Established in 2022, the Macquarie University Indian Society (MQIS) is the largest cultural student society on campus, celebrating Indian heritage through vibrant events, music, food, and community. Our mission is to create a welcoming space for Indian and non-Indian students alike to share and explore Indian traditions, festivals, and values while networking and building lifelong friendships.

From Holi and Diwali to career networking nights and campus collaborations, MQIS is a home away from home for over 800+ members and continues to grow as a dynamic, student-led community.
